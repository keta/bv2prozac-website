Babo Violent 2

Babo Violent 2 is a free multiplayer online top down 3D shooter game where you control a heavily armed orb in the fight against real players.

Modded Version

  • Enemy aim direction on the minimap
  • Direction-revealing photon beam
  • High-resolution (or simplifed) blood, smoke, flame and explosion textures
  • High-quality landscape textures (and three distraction-free packs for pro gaming)
  • Oldschool models for SMG, DMG, SR and Grenades
  • Updated interface fonts
  • Clean icons
  • Many halos to choose from (inlcluding amazing Dev's halos pack)

Now Pro Mod is completely configurable. You may choose only features you need and get your personal download.

You may, however, ask for help on one of community forums.

Q: Where have simple textures gone?

A: Simple textures are still there, but disabled by default. Open the console and type set cl_grassTextureForAllMaps true and press <Enter>. If you are in-game, you should reconnect. To enable themes back, type same command, but with false instead.
